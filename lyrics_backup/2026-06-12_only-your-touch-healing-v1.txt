[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the embers glowing when you pull me close
A quiet kind of magic that nobody knows
Your fingertips are tracing every hidden line
In this fading amber light, I know that you are mine

[Verse 1]
The midnight moon is spilling on the wooden floor
I never felt a craving pull me down before
We leave the busy highways and the dust behind
Just two shadows swaying, completely intertwined

[Pre-Chorus]
There is a secret in the way you breathe my name
It sparks a tender and a slow-burning flame
I lean in closer just to catch a falling sigh
And watch the brilliant constellations in your eye

[Chorus]
Because it is only your touch healing every broken part
Sending a heavy rhythm straight into my heart
We are drowning in the warmth of a candlelit room
Chasing the dawn away and making our own bloom

[Post-Chorus]
(Only your touch) Sweeping through the quiet air
(Only your touch) And the rhythm that we share

[Verse 2]

I hold you tighter till the shadows fade away
